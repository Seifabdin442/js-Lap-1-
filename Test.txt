Pull Request:  Is a method used to notify team members that a developer has completed a feature, bug fix, or other task and is requesting that their changes be reviewed and potentially merged into the main codebase. Pull requests are particularly common in collaborative development environments and are a key feature of platforms like GitHub, GitLab, and Bitbucket.
